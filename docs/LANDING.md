## 2. The problem

You set the goal. You meant it. Then Tuesday happened.

Most apps respond by buzzing you harder. A red streak. A guilt-colored push notification. A weekly report that ranks you against strangers. They turn missing a day into a small public failure, and they call that motivation.

The other half are just reminder lists with a logo. Neither one calls you when you're stuck at 3pm with a half-written doc and no idea where the next hour goes.

---

## 3. How Showup is different

### It calls you at every scheduled time

A real call, not a buzz. At every time you schedule — 6am gym, 9am deep work, 6pm wind-down — Showup rings and you talk to a calm AI voice coach. No tap required on Android. On iPhone, just say "Answer" and the coach is already speaking.

*Example moment:* 7:42am, your phone rings. Two minutes later you've named the one thing that matters today and you're walking to your desk.

### Frameworks, not features

Showup coaches you through real systems — Atomic Habits for daily reps, Ikigai for direction, Deep Work for focus blocks. You pick the lens per goal. The coach speaks that language instead of inventing generic advice.

*Example moment:* Your "write the book" goal runs on Deep Work. Your "call mom weekly" goal runs on Atomic Habits. Same coach, different lens.

### Built-in escape hatches

Two buttons that most apps refuse to ship. **Rough day** marks today as a real human day — no streak loss, no shame copy. **Hit a wall** triggers a rescue call mid-task to unstick you in under three minutes.

*Example moment:* 3:14pm, the doc is frozen. You tap Hit a wall. The phone rings. You're back in by 3:18.

## 4. What a day looks like

It's 7:40am. Your phone rings — automatically, no alarm to dismiss, no app to open. You answer in bed. Showup asks what today is actually about — not your whole list, just the one thing. You name it. The call ends in under three minutes and the goal is on your lock screen.

3:14pm. You're stuck on the draft. You tap Hit a wall. The phone rings back in fifteen seconds. The coach asks where the block is, you describe it out loud, and the answer surfaces while you talk. You hang up and write.

6:00pm. Gym time — your phone rings. You didn't set a reminder. Showup just knows it's gym time because you said so when you set up your goals. Two minutes of intention-setting and you're lacing your shoes.

9:50pm. One last call. What got done, what didn't, what tomorrow looks like. No scoring. No streak ceremony. You sleep with the next morning already framed.

## 6. FAQ

**Will it actually call my phone?**
Yes. On Android, your phone starts talking automatically at every scheduled time — no tap, no unlock. On iPhone, it rings with a voice cue and you say "Answer" hands-free. No chat window, no typing.

**Is this just ChatGPT in a fancy shell?**
No. Showup is built around coaching frameworks (Atomic Habits, Ikigai, Deep Work at launch), one consistent voice persona across all your goals, and product surfaces — the rough-day button, the wall-rescue call, the morning ring — that a chatbot can't replicate.

**What if I have a rough day?**
Tap Rough day. The day is logged as a real human day. You keep your streak, you skip the evening call, and tomorrow's morning call starts soft instead of stern.

**Can I cancel anytime?**
Yes. One-tap account delete removes every row tied to your account. Showup is free during beta, so there's nothing to cancel yet — when paid plans launch you'll be able to pause coaching without losing your goals or history.

**Does it work without my calendar?**
Yes. Calendar sync is optional and adds context (your 2pm block is busy, so the rescue window is 3pm). Without it, Showup works off the goals you set and the times you pick for calls.

**Who's behind this?**
A small team that got tired of habit apps that felt like guilt machines. We're shipping in public — the privacy pledge is binding, the roadmap is visible, and the beta is open.
