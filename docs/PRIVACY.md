# Showup — Privacy Pledge

This is a commitment, not a marketing page. It binds the product. Anything we collect, store, or send to a vendor must conform to what's listed here.

## What we collect

- **Goals** you set in the app.
- **Voice transcripts** of check-in calls (auto-purged after 90 days unless you explicitly opt to keep).
- **Calendar events** — **title and time only**. We never read attendees, descriptions, locations, or attachments.
- **Task events** (started, completed, skipped, etc.) — generated by your own actions or check-in outcomes.
- **Device push token** so we can ring you for a check-in.

## What we never do

- We never **sell** your data.
- We never **train** any model on your data (ours or a vendor's).
- We never **manually review** your transcripts or goals. Engineering access to production data is logged and gated.
- We never store calendar **attendees**, **descriptions**, or **attachments**. The sync function discards them at the source.

## Where it lives

- **Supabase** (Postgres) — your data, encrypted at rest. Access is RLS-gated by your auth user; no admin "see everyone" view exists.
- **Google** (Gemini Live) — voice and reasoning are unified in one native-audio inference path. Your voice and goal context are sent at inference time. Google's DPA covers no-train and short retention.

## Controls you have

- **One-tap delete account.** Hard delete of every row keyed to your `auth.uid()`.
- **Transcript opt-in keep.** Default is purge at 90 days. Explicit toggle per-call to keep longer.
- **Calendar disconnect.** Removes the OAuth token, deletes the `calendar_events` mirror.
- **Pause coaching.** Suppresses check-ins without losing your goals or history.

## What we'll tell you if it changes

- New vendor: announced in the app and via email before any data leaves to it.
- Any change to "what we never do": pinned in-app for 14 days before taking effect.
- Breach: notified within 72 hours per applicable law.
